About Me

Hi, my name is Zesty Fernandez (ZestyTS). I am the developer of The MisAdventures of Xenos, an action RPG adventure available on multiple platforms. I also manage the development of UWUVCI V3, a Wii U Virtual Console injections tool. Additionally, I run a YouTube channel called Zesty's Corner, where I share content about technology, gaming, and development.

If you need support for something of mine that you paid for, please email me at ZestyTS@gmail.com with the name of the product in the subject followed by "Support" and describe your issue. I will get back to you as soon as possible. Feel free to reach out for business inquiries.
